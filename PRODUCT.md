# PRODUCT.md — PatchMood

register: brand

## Product purpose

PatchMood is a browse-only showcase for a collection of mood-pieces: garments engineered around a single feeling. The site is the product. A visitor should leave having *felt* the collection, not just seen a catalog. There is no cart and no checkout; the job is atmosphere and desire, not transaction.

The experience is built around a cinematic scroll-driven video and a curated grid of six moods. The new direction borrows the visual language of an underground collector's loft: a dim industrial space where rare objects are lit and labeled like gallery pieces.

## Users

Obsessive aesthetes. People who care about the *mood* of an object more than its utility, who collect feelings the way others collect sneakers or die-cast cars. They browse late, on dark screens, looking for the one piece that matches a state of mind.

## Brand voice

Three words: **brutal, curated, nocturnal.**

Confident and sparse. Short declarative lines. Industrial confidence, not luxury softness. Copy reads like wall-text in a concept gallery, not like a fashion ad. Never cute, never apologetic.

## Aesthetic direction

A brutalist concept-store loft after midnight. Warm concrete near-black, lit by Ferrari-red neon and the colored glow of the pieces themselves. Heavy condensed uppercase headlines stacked like poster type. Thin hairline-outlined cards that float over a dark cinematic scene, each labeled and numbered like an inventory item.

Lane: brutalist-industrial / poster-maximalist. Explicitly NOT editorial-serif. The previous Cormorant-italic identity is retired.

## Anti-references

- Soft luxury fashion (no thin serifs, no beige, no airy whitespace minimalism).
- Generic SaaS or e-commerce templates (no centered icon-title-subtitle card stacks).
- Editorial-magazine affectation (display serif + italic + ruled columns). We left that lane on purpose.
- Glassmorphism, gradient text, hero-metric blocks.

## Strategic principles

- The scroll-video hero is the centerpiece and stays. Everything overlays it; nothing replaces it.
- Color comes from the pieces. The chrome around them stays charcoal and red.
- Type carries the brutalism: oversized condensed caps do the heavy lifting, not decoration.
- Out of scope: cart, checkout, orders, payments, reviews, profiles, i18n.
